Accend

AI-Powered Speech Improvement

Learn to speak, not just study. Accend gives real-time pronunciation feedback and phoneme-level analysis to help you sound natural, confident, and understood.

The Problem

Why pronunciation learning is broken

Most language apps grade you on vocabulary and grammar but skip the hardest part: actually sounding correct. The ones that do cover pronunciation rely on pre-recorded audio clips and pass/fail checks that can't tell you where your mouth went wrong. You repeat the same mistakes without knowing it.

The Solution

Feedback that actually helps

Accend uses phoneme-level analysis to score every syllable of every attempt. You see exactly which sounds missed and by how much. Pair that with live group sessions where real people hear you and practice alongside you. You no longer need to pay for a speech therapist or keep asking friends and coworkers to tell you if you sound right. The correction that used to cost hundreds of dollars an hour now fits in your pocket.

Accend isn’t just for people learning English — it’s for anyone who wants their spoken words to land exactly as intended.
